RR


I am a third year Graphic Communication major at California Polytechnic University in San Luis Obispo.

I am interested in the fields of design, web, digital photography, and videography. Ever since I was a child, my interests have been in creative fields, starting with drawing. Since picking up a pen for the first time, I have moved into the realm of digital creation and now embrace technology and the advantages it brings to design.

In my free time I enjoy rock climbing, yoga, and spending time outside. Music is another huge aspect of my life, and something I draw creative inspiration from constantly. Working for my college radio station, KCPR, has helped me to delve even deeper into the industry and further my knowledge of music.
